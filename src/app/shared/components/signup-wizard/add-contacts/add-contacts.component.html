What is Durity ?

A digital vault that securely stores valuable information and passes it on to your loved ones in an emergency.

 Your data is super secure with Durity

Durity encrypts all your confidential files before they are uploaded to the cloud applying industry-standard highest security 256-bit AES encryption algorithm.

Due to the secure encryption methods used to store your data, no one can see what you have uploaded. Your vault is completely private.

We are using leading Encryption technologies to secure your data and protect user privacy. Our proven security model sets the standard for transparency and best practices.
