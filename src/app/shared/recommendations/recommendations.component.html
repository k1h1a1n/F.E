These are just recommendations for the levels.You may assign levels according to your wish

Beneficiary(L1):
e.g:Spouse
Beneficiary(L1) trustee can claim the documents in-case of emergency. If there is no surviving Beneficiary(L1), then Beneficiary(L2) / Guardian/Trustee can claim the document.
Beneficiary(L2):
e.g:Children(If Major)
Parents (Or) Siblings
(if your children are minor)
Beneficiary(L2) will be the recipient of your document in the absence of Beneficiary(L1).
Guardian/Trustee:
e.g:Parents, In-laws, Siblings C.A,
Lawyer
Guardian/Trustee will be the recipient of your document in the absence of Beneficiary(L1) / Beneficiary(L2).
